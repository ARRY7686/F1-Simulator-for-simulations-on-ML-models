Playback
SpacePlay / Pause the simulation
← / →Rewind / skip 30 seconds
1 – 5Set playback speed (1×, 5×, 15×, 30×, 60×)
ScrubberClick or drag the bar at the bottom to jump to any race moment
Following a Driver
Click carClick any car dot on the track to follow it — camera locks on
Click nameClick a driver row in the standings to follow them
EscExit follow mode and return to the full-track view
✕ badgeClick the driver badge (top-left) to stop following
Live Standings
OrderDrivers sorted by race position in real-time
GapTime gap to the car ahead shown on each row
TyresCurrent compound shown as a coloured dot per driver
PittingRow flashes when a driver is in the pit lane
RetiredDimmed rows (opacity 45%) indicate retired drivers
BattleAmber pulse on rows within 1.5 s of each other
